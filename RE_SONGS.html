RE's FAVE SONGS

  • "Blue Light" by Kelela

    KELELA · Blue Light

    "Blue Light" is the second single for Kelela's 2017 debut album, Take Me Apart. I really love this song because it is unconventional and passionate, thanks to Kelela's stellar vocals and lyrics.

    KELELA Official Website

  • "Cocoa Butter Kisses" by Chance the Rapper

    "Chance The Rapper" · Cocoa Butter Kisses (ft. Vic Mensa & Twista)

    "Cocoa Butter Kisses" is originally the third track off of Chance the Rapper's 2013 breakout mixtape, Acid Rap. This is such a gem of a song to me because it is deeply soulful and melancholy, and every verse is top-tier lyricism.

    Chance the Rapper Official Website

  • "Homecoming" by Kanye West

    "Homecoming" is the final hit single from Kanye West's 2007 classic album, Graduation. As a huge Ye fan, I would say this is my most favorite song of his. From the piano-led production to Kanye rapping passionate verses alongside Chris Martin about his hometown of Chicago, truly what is there not to love about this track?

    Kanye West Official Website
